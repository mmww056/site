How to set up your layout »

1. Open the PSD file in your photo-editing software (GIMP, Photoshop, etc.) and make the necessary changes to the layout.
2. Upload the layout to Tinypic, Photobucket, etc.
3. Copy the direct URL of the layout.
4. In the panel code, locate the orange highlighted part #1 (refer to the image below) and replace it with the URL you just copied.
5. In the panel code, locate the orange highlighted part #2 (refer to the image below) and replace my IMVU username with yours.
6. In the panel code, locate the orange highlighted parts #3 and #4 (refer to the image below) and replace my IMVU account number with your account number. If you do not know what your account number is, go to the Account page and look for "Member Number" in the "Member Profile" section.



7. Press CTRL+F, and then type "about me" (without the quotation marks). You should see that the entire line says, "ABOUT ME AND AVATAR PICTURE SECTION BEGINS BELOW." Below this line, you should see my IMVU username. Replace my username with yours.
8. Now, replace all of the "text text text" with your own text. Be sure to save your progress.
9. When finished, select all of the text in the panel code file and paste it to your URL panel.
